**Tigris is a Serverless NoSQL Database and Search Platform that offers a modern
open source alternative to MongoDB and DynamoDB.** Tigris allows developers to
rapidly build applications with a NoSQL platform that combines database,
search, and sync mechanism. Tigris simplifies operations by
automatically scaling throughput and storage as application traffic grows, at
fraction of the cost of MongoDB and DynamoDB, while providing high availability
and data security.
