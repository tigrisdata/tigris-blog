## LinkedIn

For years, Tigris has handled global distribution automatically. That works great until your architecture or compliance requirements need tighter control.

Today, we’re introducing **Multi-Region and Dual-Region Buckets** so you can choose exactly where data lives and how it replicates:
- **Global** for worldwide reads
- **Multi-region** (`usa` / `eur`) as the default for resilient production workloads
- **Dual-region** when you need to pin to exact regions
- **Single-region** for cost-efficient local workloads

The important shift: you no longer have to choose between predictable data residency and operational simplicity. You get both, without building your own replication layer.

If your team is balancing latency, resilience, and residency constraints, this is for you.

Full announcement: https://www.tigrisdata.com/blog/multi-region-dual-region-buckets
