Sowing your dataset in object storage with many small files leaves you with many small problems when it comes to reap them. Why can't the nerds that make these things just let you download multiple files at once?

Hi, it's us, we're Tigris and we're the nerds that let you do that with object bundles. Object bundles take your objects and shove them into one big lump of data so you can download thousands of objects in a single API call. No more having to pay enterprise subscriptions to CyberDuck.

We didn't just stop at making it easy for your dataloaders, we also made it easy for you to use on the web. Check it out! This demo is legitimately fast enough that it looks fake! This is after I artificially slowed down Firefox too.
